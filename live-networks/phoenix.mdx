[Phoenix](https://phoenix.lightlink.io) is the main network of LightLink. It is connected to the
Ethereum mainnet and is the first L2 leveraging Optimum architecture with its proprietary stack.
This enables LightLink to achieve high throughput at under $0.01, without gas spikes.
