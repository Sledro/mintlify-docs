[Phoenix](https://phoenix.lightlink.io) is the main network of LightLink. It is connected to the Ethereum mainnet and is the first L2 leveraging Optimum architecture with its proprietary stack,
which separates the consensus, execution, and data availability layers. This enables it to reach 10,000+ TPS at under $0.01, without gas spikes.
